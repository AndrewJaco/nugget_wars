About Nugget Wars

Nugget wars is a project designed by PPDW (paperplane Design Workshop). Nugget wars is a collection of NFTs (non-fungible tokens) created in February 2022. Nugget wars consists of 10,000 images of rebellious chicken nuggets. All chicken nuggets have different type traits, which are the design elements that make up the character. From a king's crown to a ninja outfit to a space suit, the nuggets are all different, but they have one thing in common: they're rebellious.

The biggest feature of Nugget wars is that PPDW designed a special game mechanism throughout the project. The chicken nuggets fought against humans in order to escape from the frying pan of the fast food restaurant. The chicken nuggets would inevitably be caught by humans in the process of escaping and building a country. All buyers have the opportunity to "UNMINT" them after purchasing chicken nuggets, which means that the chicken nuggets are recaptured and fried to produce new chicken nuggets.
